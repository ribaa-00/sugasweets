Our Story

From a small family kitchen to your favorite neighborhood bakery.

Founded in 1985 by master baker Pierre LeBlanc, Golden Crust Bakery started as a passion project in a small home kitchen. Today, we continue Pierre's tradition of using only the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques.

Our team of artisan bakers wakes before dawn every day to ensure you get the freshest breads, pastries, and desserts. We source our ingredients locally whenever possible and never use artificial preservatives.

Pierre LeBlanc
Master Baker & Founder
